Embroidery Pattern Designer - Help

Step-By-Step Instructions

  1. Check "Add Mode" and then click on the canvas at the desired location to add each new stitch.
  2. Uncheck "Add Mode" and select any stitch by clicking on it.
  3. Move a selected stitch with drag and drop.
  4. Delete a selected stitch by pressing "delete" or clicking the "Delete Selected Stitch" button.

Helpful Tips

  • Add as many stitches as you want to. That is how you draw your pattern.
  • Use drag and drop stitch movement or deletion to fix pattern problems without starting over.
  • Use the Undo and Redo buttons to recover changes
  • Want more information about the selected a stitch? Check "Show Stitch Info" to get more detailed information.
